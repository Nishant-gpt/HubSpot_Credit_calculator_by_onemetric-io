How credits work

What are HubSpot AI Credits?

AI Credits are the currency for HubSpot's AI-powered features. Each AI action (conversation, workflow step, data sync) consumes a set number of credits based on complexity.

How are credits included in my plan?

Your monthly credit allocation depends on your highest-tier Smart CRM/Marketing/Sales/Service/Content Hub subscription. Data Hub or Customer Platform tiers add additional credits on top.

What happens when I run out of credits?

You have two options: purchase Capacity Packs (1,000 credits for $10) or use pay-as-you-go Overage billing ($0.01 per credit, minimum 10-credit blocks). We recommend the most cost-effective option automatically.

Do unused credits roll over?

No. Included credits reset monthly. Only purchased Capacity Pack credits may have different terms‚Äîconfirm with your HubSpot CSM.
